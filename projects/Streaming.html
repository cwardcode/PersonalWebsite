Data Collecting Downsides

Since the advent of field data loggers, there has always been one problem -- getting the data into scientists hands to analyze. Having multiple loggers in potentially different locations can be a hassle when it comes to actually collecting the logged data, requiring someone to go out to the field with a laptop and plugging in to get their precious data. Why can't this process be automated, specifically for the Campbell Scientific series of Dataloggers?
