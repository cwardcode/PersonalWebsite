MIPS/Assembly

Why knowing Assembly is useful

It's the basis of all languages, for a start.

Understanding any version of assembly is essential to have a proper understanding of computers. When learning how assembly works, one must first understand how computers organize and process data. This leads to a greater understanding of computers themselves.

Also, let's admit it. If it wasn't for assembly coding would be a nightmare. Just imagine programming in machine code. Ack!.
